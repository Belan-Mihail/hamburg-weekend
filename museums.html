Mineralogisches Museum

go to the next museum

An amazing museum dedicated to unusual stones and minerals. Despite the small area, the exposition is represented by hundreds of unusual exhibits. Here you can see minerals of the most amazing colors and shapes, precious metals and stones, and even fragments of space meteorites collected in Germany. This museum is a great place for everyone who wants to see something new and unusual, as well as for those who are not indifferent to science.

Miniatur Wunderland

go to the previous museum go to the next museum

One of the most famous museums not only in Hamburg but throughout Germany. Thousands of square meters of territory filled with platforms with Lego structures. At the same time, Lego structures are always interactive. visitors are allowed to press the buttons at the platforms, after which the action on the platform comes to life: the train starts to move, fireworks explode, a volcano erupts and even a plane takes off. It will take many hours to see all the locations of the museum. The scale and detail of the exposure is simply incredible. You will truly enjoy your visit.
